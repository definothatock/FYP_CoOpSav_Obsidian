# THE BAD

### Building several games at once, while still learning on the fly
We tried to build:
- Collections of cooperative mini puzzles
- Unique AI engagements
- Unique Item collections
- Unique Hazards Evasion
Where all of them can directly be a game of its own (all actually exist), and still have enough depth to find sets of audience. Combining sub-games to enrich main-game experiences is not inherently unfeasible, but as a team of 4 students who are learning on the fly, while still studying in school, we can hardly make sure all sub-games are polished.
